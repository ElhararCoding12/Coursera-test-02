    Tacos

    Why are tacos so popular? Tacos have become so popular they now rival the hamburger as one of America's most popular food staples. ... “Taco” was actually the word miners in the 18th century used to describe the small charges they used to evacuate ore (hence the belief that miners created a dish they called a taco).

    Tacos are thought to come from Mexico, long before the Spanish arrived. Ancient Mexicans used freshly made, soft, flat corn tortillas and gave them with fillings like fish and cooked organs. It was a staple meal that provided vital nutrients and energy to those who consumed it.

    Why do you like tacos? Tacos are cheap, delicious, nutritious, can be found in every corner, they are varied, you can make them fit any taste, they can be big or small, spicy or sweet, plus they are part of our identify and history. That is why some people love tacos so much.
